Lingomon is an educational broswer extension for catching and learning new words!

Bright popups, tier systems and more will make learning new words a fun journey.

The system uses word length + probabilistic assignment to determine rarity:

Tier 1: COMMON #ebebeb

  - Words in predefined common word list (the, and, is, have, etc.) → Always common
  - ≤5 letters: 85% chance
  - 6-7 letters: 65% chance
  - 8-10 letters: 30% chance

  Examples:
  - hello (5 letters)
  - world (5 letters)
  - people (6 letters)
  - reading (7 letters)

  ---
  Tier 2: UNCOMMON #a1ff96 (pastel green)

  - ≤5 letters: 15% chance
  - 6-7 letters: 23% chance
  - 8-10 letters: 25% chance
  - 11-13 letters: 10% chance

  Examples:
  - quirk (5 letters)
  - puzzle (6 letters)
  - chapter (7 letters)
  - beautiful (9 letters)

  ---
  Tier 3: RARE #96c7ff (pastel blue)

  - 6-7 letters: 10% chance
  - 8-10 letters: 23% chance
  - 11-13 letters: 25% chance

  Examples:
  - zealous (7 letters)
  - paradigm (8 letters)
  - algorithm (9 letters)
  - magnificent (11 letters)

  ---
  Tier 4: EPIC #b996ff (pastel purple)

  - 6-7 letters: 2% chance
  - 8-10 letters: 15% chance
  - 11-13 letters: 30% chance
  - 14+ letters: 20% chance

  Examples:
  - epitome (7 letters - rare drop!)
  - ephemeral (9 letters)
  - serendipity (11 letters)
  - philosophical (13 letters)

  ---
  Tier 5: LEGENDARY #fffa96 (pastel yellow)

  - 8-10 letters: 6% chance
  - 11-13 letters: 25% chance
  - 14+ letters: 40% chance

  Examples:
  - zeitgeist (9 letters - very rare!)
  - nomenclature (12 letters)
  - infrastructure (14 letters)
  - phenomenological (16 letters)

  ---
  Tier 6: MYTHIC #ff6969 (pastel pink/red)

  - 8-10 letters: 1% chance (ultra rare!)
  - 11-13 letters: 10% chance
  - 14+ letters: 40% chance

  Examples:
  - Byzantine (9 letters - extremely rare drop!)
  - uncomfortable (13 letters)
  - uncharacteristically (20 letters)
  - antidisestablishmentarianism (28 letters - almost guaranteed!)
